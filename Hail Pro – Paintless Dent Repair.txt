Hailpro PDR Solutions LLC is a company specialized in hail damage repair for vehicles, based at 1440 Westpark Way, Suite H, Euless, Texas, 76040, United States.

Founded in 2024 by Maylon da Cruz and Jonatas Alves, the company was built with a clear vision: to elevate industry standards through technical excellence, professionalism, and consistent results.


WHAT IS PDR?

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is an advanced technique that allows the repair of dents caused by hail without the need for painting, fillers, or invasive processes.

Through specialized tools and millimeter precision, we restore the vehicle to its original shape while preserving the factory paint.

This ensures:

Higher vehicle value
Faster results
Premium-quality finish

Meet the Team

Maylon da Cruz
CEO and PDR Expert
Born in Brazil, Maylon moved to Portugal with his family at the age of 15. Growing up with an entrepreneurial father, he developed early on a strong mindset focused on building, growth, and independence.

In 2016, his first son was born — a life-changing moment that marked a new level of responsibility and purpose. That same year, he launched his first automotive detailing business, beginning his journey as a business owner in the automotive industry.

Over the years, he expanded his expertise into Paintless Dent Repair (PDR), working on projects across multiple international markets including Spain, Switzerland, France, Dubai, and Australia. This global experience allowed him to refine his skills, adapt to different environments, and operate at a high professional standard.

In 2024, he co-founded Hailpro PDR Solutions, turning a long-term vision into reality through discipline, consistency, and faith.

His mindset is clear: when you have a strong vision, the next step is execution. He believes success is built through hard work, consistency, and staying focused without compromising core values.

Today, he is driven by commitment, ambition, and the determination to take his company to the highest level — always grounded in discipline, responsibility, and excellence.

Jonatas Alves
CEO and PDR Expert

Born in Brazil, Jonatas moved to Spain at the age of 15, where he completed his education and began building his professional path.

From an early stage, he was connected to the Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) industry through his family. His cousin, already established in the field, introduced him to the trade and provided the foundation that would later shape his career and future.

With over 10 years of experience, he has developed a strong background in the automotive sector, working on international projects and continuously refining both his technical and operational skills.

In 2024, he became a co-founder of Hailpro PDR Solutions, taking on a key role in the company’s operations and growth.

He is known for his commitment, consistency, and ability to add value at every stage of the process. For him, family is a fundamental pillar, and that foundation is reflected in his work ethic, responsibility, and drive for continuous improvement.

His focus is clear: to grow every day, contribute to the team, and help build a strong, efficient, and results-driven company.
